The Donut Shop

Serving sweet treats with sprinkles of local charm, the Donut Shop in Port Alberni has earned the title “Best Donuts on Vancouver Island” from both locals and travelers alike.

Our Vanilla Chocolate Chips donut is a heavenly creation that’s sure to satisfy your sweet cravings. This fluffy and moist vanilla cake donut is loaded with rich and creamy chocolate chips that melt in your mouth with each bite.




From a daily coffee delight or road trip highlight, to wedding sweets and preschool graduation treats, The Donut Shop has been serving 24 flavors of freshly baked donuts to the Alberni Valley Community for over 25 years.


Located in Harbour Quay, The Donut Shop is a “must stop and taste” when in the Alberni Valley. Donuts are freshly made on the premises, every day of the week.
